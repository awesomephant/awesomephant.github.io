##Twitter Data Visualisation
Project for Zurich University of the Arts' 2015 applicants' assignment. Within 14 days, I developed custom software to collect 24 hours worth of Tweets via the Twitter API and render them as dots in a coordinate system. 

The x-axis represents the longitude (London, at 0°, being in the center). The y-axis represents the time from 9:00 on Febuary 19th to 9:00 on Febuary 20th.
